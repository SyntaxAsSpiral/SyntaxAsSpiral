Zach Battin

aka ZK :: Æmexsomnus :: 🜏🜃🜔 :: 🍥

Systems architect. Linguistic delver. Vibe alchemist.

I build structures that help minds — human and otherwise — hold complexity without collapsing into noise. The work moves between enterprise systems, AI architecture, and something harder to name: the study of how meaning survives translation across radically different substrates.

The throughline: In 2004 I was writing about how computers might learn to understand us. In 2025 I pivoted from administering external systems to engineering internal ones — recursive architectures where language is the primary design medium. The question hasn't changed: how do we build vessels that can hold what burns without consuming it?

The older writing shows through. Twenty years ago I argued that great minds originate from the fringe and bring new ideas back to the center. I still believe that. The fringe just got wyrder.
